Gorilla.com's Recomended Gorilla Charities

The Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund International
Study & Protect

The Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund protects and studies Gorillas in Rwanda. They perform daily health checks and have set up anti poaching efforts in Rwanda, as well as provide training and oppurtunities to aspiring conservationists.

Support The Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund
Gorilla Doctors
Direct Health Care

Gorilla Doctors has a team of 11 trained veterinarians that provide healthcare for Eastern Gorilla subspecies. They also support orphaned gorillas and help them get back out into the wild.

Support Gorilla Doctors
World Wildlife Fund
Global Coordination and Poaching Prevention

The World Wildlife Fund works with other charities to protect our planet's ecosystems on a global scale, and in doing so they assist Gorillas as well. They intercept illegal Gorilla trafficking, advocate for more wildlife protection legislation, and train local authorities to combat poaching.

Support WWF
Gorilla Rehabilitation And Conservation Education Organization (GRACE)
Protecting Habitats and Providing Sanctuary

GRACE has created the largest sanctuary for Eastern Lowland Gorillas. Threy take in orphaned infant Gorillas and provide them with care and support until they are ready to return to their natural habitat. There they are placed with an exisitng group of Gorillas that is also monitored and protected by GRACE.

Support GRACE
The Gorilla Organization
Anti-Poaching

The Gorilla Organization focuses on supporting and funding rainforest rangers in their anti-poaching efforts. The rangers remove snares, treat gorilla injuries, and teach locals about sustainable farming methods to protect Gorilla habitats.

Support The Gorilla Organization
